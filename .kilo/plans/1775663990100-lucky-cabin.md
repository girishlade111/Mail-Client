### 8. UX Guidelines

1. **Panel Width:** ~320px (right side drawer)
2. **Tab Navigation:** Icons with labels, active state
3. **Compact Lists:** Scrollable content areas
4. **Quick Actions:** Icon buttons for common actions
5. **Hover States:** Clear interaction feedback
6. **Empty States:** Helpful messages when no content
7. **Mobile:** Panel becomes full-screen bottom sheet
